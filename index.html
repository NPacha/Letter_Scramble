    Instructions

    Each team will be presented with 10 letters. Your goal is to come up with as many words as you can using only letters from this list, in 30 seconds.

    Two letter words = 10 points
    Three letter words = 20 points
    Four letter words = 30 points
    Five or more letter words = 40 points

    Need to check a word from the other team? Click the dispute button to argue this word and check the dictionary.

    If the word is not legitimate, use the trash icon to remove the words and points.

    First team to make 300 points wins!
