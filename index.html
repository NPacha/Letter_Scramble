    Letter Scramble Instructions

    You will be given 7 letters at random. The goal is to come up with as many words as you can in 15 secs.Each word is worth 10 points. The first person to reach 500 points wins! Don't forget to shuffle the words each time it's a new teams turn. If you believe that a word that a team wrote is not valid, click the dispute button to check in the dictionary! If not valid, you can delete from the list.
